Itinerary

  • 12:00pm - The convention begins with the Opening Ceremony/Kickoff. All channels marked for convention use will open for posting and the all-day anime stream will begin (see anime schedule below.
  • 6:00pm - Panels begin (see schedule below).
  • 12:00am - The Closing Ceremony begins.
  • 12:10am - The convention ends with the ending of the closing ceremony. All channels marked for convention use will close.

Panel Schedule

  • 12:00pm - 12:10pm — Opening
    The LightCon Staff are delighted to welcome you to our first convention! We hope you have fun.
    Panelists: LightCon Staff
  • 6:00pm - 7:00pm — AMV Exhibition
    A selection of recent AMVs from editors both well-known and new, featuring a variety of great music and anime series.
    Panelists: Jessi Silver
  • 7:30pm - 8:30pm — The Best Anime You've (Maybe) Never Heard Of
    An overview of some lesser-known anime series for fans who are interested in digging up some hidden gems.
    Panelists: Jessi Silver, J.C. Horvath
  • 9:00pm - 10:00pm — Hold on to Your Smuts! (18+)
    Do you crave crafting fervent feelings into a salacious scene that grabs readers? Here, we'll talk about how to write strong smut and workshop some writing (workshop is optional).
    Panelists: Deka-nee
    NOTE: This panel is rated for ages 18+ and requires age-verification to access; see #read-to-access-r18 in the Discord server for more info on accessing that section of the server.
  • 12:00am - 12:10am — Closing
    The LightCon Staff wish you a good night, and a preview of what the future of LightCon will bring.
    Panelists: LightCon Staff
